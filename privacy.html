Privacy Policy

 Effective Date: January 1, 2025

Welcome to QuickChart. This Privacy Policy describes how QuickChart ("we," "our," or "us") handles information. We have designed this Application with a privacy-first approach specifically for the sensitive nature of EMS documentation.

1. Information Collection: None

QuickChart does not collect, transmit, or store any information on external servers. We do not collect personally identifiable information (PII), Protected Health Information (PHI), or aggregated usage analytics. Any data you enter remains exclusively on your device.

2. How Your Information is Processed

All processing of narratives, facility lists, and patient data occurs locally within your browser. Because we do not receive your data, we cannot use, see, sell, or share it with any third parties.

3. Local Browser Storage

The Application utilizes local browser storage (such as localStorage or IndexedDB) to save your settings and documentation drafts.

  • Control: This data never leaves your device.
  • Deletion: Clearing your browser cache or "Site Data" will permanently delete any stored information.
  • Security: You are responsible for maintaining the physical and digital security of your device to prevent unauthorized access to this local storage.

4. HIPAA and Regulatory Compliance

Because QuickChart does not "create, receive, maintain, or transmit" PHI on behalf of a covered entity (it merely provides a local tool for the user to do so), QuickChart does not function as a Business Associate as defined by HIPAA. Users are responsible for ensuring their use of the Application aligns with their employer's specific data privacy and retention policies.

5. Third-Party Links

This Application does not contain third-party trackers or advertisements. Any links provided within the app are for user convenience only.

6. Changes to This Policy

As the Software evolves, we may update this policy to reflect changes in how local data is handled. The "Effective Date" at the top will reflect the most recent version.
