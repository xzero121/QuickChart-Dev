Privacy Policy

 Effective Date: May 23, 2025

Welcome to QuickChart. This Privacy Policy describes how QuickChart ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and discloses information gathered from users ("you") through the QuickChart web application ("Application"). By using the Application, you consent to the terms described in this policy.

Information We Collect

We collect only aggregated, non-personally identifiable information related to specific fields, such as facility names and similar general, non-sensitive information. We do not collect or store personally identifiable information (PII) or Protected Health Information (PHI).

How We Use Your Information

The aggregated data collected is used exclusively to enhance and improve the lists and suggestions provided to users within the Application. This helps streamline the user experience and improve efficiency in creating narratives.

Data Storage and Security

All aggregated data collected is stored securely using industry-standard methods and practices to prevent unauthorized access or disclosure. No individual user can be identified from the aggregated data we collect.

Information Sharing

We do not share, sell, or disclose the aggregated data collected to third parties except as required by law.

Cookies and Local Storage

The Application utilizes local browser storage (localStorage or sessionStorage) to save your settings and narrative content for convenience. This data remains exclusively on your device and is never transmitted or collected by QuickChart.

User Rights

As we do not collect personally identifiable information, we do not provide access, correction, or deletion rights related to individual user data.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy periodically. Users will be notified of any significant changes through updates posted within the Application.

Contact Us

For any questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us at bbrown216@protonmail.com.

Disclaimer: Data is stored in your browser's local storage. Clearing browser data will remove saved narrative. No information is sent to a server.
